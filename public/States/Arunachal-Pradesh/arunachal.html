Discover the Beauty of Arunachal Pradesh

Explore the rich heritage, spirituality, and adventure of Arunachal Pradesh

Heritage Sites

Explore the historical wonders of Arunachal Pradesh, including The Tawang Monastery, Bhismaknagar Fort, and more.

  • Tawang Monastery, one of the largest monasteries in India

  • Bhismaknagar Fort, an archaeological site with historical significance

  • Itanagar, the state capital with attractions like Ita Fort and Jawaharlal Nehru State Museum

Spiritual Journeys

Embark on spiritual adventures to Parshuram Kund, Urgelling Monastery, and other sacred places.

  • Parshuram Kund, a revered pilgrimage site.

  • Urgelling Monastery, the birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama.

  • Malinithan Temple, dedicated to Goddess Parvati.

Cultural Experiences

Losar Festival, a major festival celebrated by the Monpa tribe, Nyishi Tribe's traditional dances and rituals, Explore the vibrant local markets for traditional handicrafts

  • Culinary Delights

    • Soups & veggies

      Thukpa, a noodle soup with meat or vegetables

    • Local famous foods

      Bamboo Shoot and Pork Curry, a local delicacy

    • Apong

      Apong, a traditional rice beer

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Popir & Wancho

      Enjoy traditional dances like Popir, Wancho, and Buiya

    • Traditional folk

      Listen to traditional music with instruments like bamboo flutes and drums

  • Traditional Handicrafts

    • Unique Textiles

      Purchase colorful handwoven textiles and carpets

    • Crafting & Carvings

      Admire intricate wood carvings and bamboo crafts

    • Beautiful Paintings

      Thangka paintings depicting Buddhist themes

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Losar

      The Tibetan New Year celebrated with traditional music and dance.

    • Torgya

      A Monpa festival featuring mask dances and religious rituals.

    • Nyokum Yullo

      The main festival of the Nyishi tribe, celebrated with traditional dances and games.

    • Sangken

      The Water Festival celebrated by the Khampti community.

    • Solung

      Adi tribe's harvest festival with various rituals and community feasting.
