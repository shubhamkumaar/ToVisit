Spiritual Journeys

Embark on spiritual adventures to most famous Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, and other sacred places.

  • Visit the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram

  • Explore the Guruvayur Temple dedicated to Lord Krishna

  • Experience the tranquility of the Sabarimala Temple

  • Seek blessings at the Attukal Bhagavathy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram

Cultural Experiences

Attend the traditional Kerala Kathakali and Mohiniyattam dance, Witness Theyyam and Thiruvathira dance forms, Explore the Kerala Folklore Museum in Kochi, Participate in Onam celebrations and boat races

  • Culinary Delights

    • Kerala-cuisine

      Savor Kerala's traditional cuisine with dishes like Kerala Sadya, Appam, and Puttu

    • Varieties in seafoods

      Try seafood delicacies like Karimeen Pollichathu (fish) and Chemmeen Ularthiyathu (prawns)

    • Savoury deserts

      Enjoy Payasam and Banana Chips for dessert

    • Coconut

      Relish the flavors of coconut in every meal

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Thayambaka

      Experience the energetic rhythms of Thayambaka and Panchari Melam.

    • Kerala Folk

      Enjoy Kerala's folk songs, including Mappila Pattu and Oppana.

    • Art forms

      Watch traditional art forms like Kalaripayattu martial arts.

    • Melodious Musics

      Listen to classical music forms like Carnatic and Sopana Sangeetham.

  • Traditional Handicrafts

    • Fabrics & Textiles

      Shop for intricate Kerala handloom fabrics, especially Kasavu sarees.

    • Handicrafts

      Explore the vibrant markets for Kathakali masks and handicrafts

    • Ornaments

      Buy Nettipattom (elephant ornaments) and Aranmula Kannadi (metal mirrors)

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Onam

      Kerala's biggest harvest festival, celebrated with elaborate feasts, cultural performances, and the traditional dance, Kathakali.

    • Thrissur Pooram

      Known for its grand elephant procession, this festival showcases impressive fireworks and musical performances in Thrissur.

    • Vishu

      Kerala's New Year celebration, marked by the "Vishukkani" arrangement of auspicious items and giving gifts to family members.

    • Theyyam Festival

      Unique ritualistic dances and performances that depict ancient folklore and traditions, primarily in Northern Kerala.

    • Attukal Pongala

      The world's largest congregation of women for a religious ritual, where they cook a special offering called "Pongala" in Trivandrum.
