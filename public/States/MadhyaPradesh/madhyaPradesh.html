About

Madhya Pradesh (MP), located in central India, is a state of diverse landscapes and rich history. Bhopal serves as its capital, and the state's culture reflects a mix of Hindu, Jain, and tribal traditions. MP's economy relies on agriculture and industry, with prominent tourist destinations like Khajuraho and national parks. It faces challenges in rural development and healthcare, but educational institutions like IIT and IIM Indore are contributing to its growth. Governed by a parliamentary system, MP offers a blend of tradition and modernity for study and exploration.

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Khajuraho Dance Festival

      This festival takes place in February and showcases classical Indian dances against the backdrop of the stunning Khajuraho temples.

    • Tansen Music Festival:

      Held in December in Gwalior, this festival celebrates the legendary musician Tansen with classical music performances.

    • Ujjain Kumbh Mela

      Ujjain hosts the Kumbh Mela every 12 years, and it's a major pilgrimage for Hindus. It's a massive gathering of devotees who take a holy dip in the River Kshipra.

    • Mandu Festival

      This festival, usually held in March, celebrates the heritage of Mandu with cultural performances, light and sound shows, and more.

    • Pachmarhi Utsav

      It's an annual festival held in Pachmarhi, a hill station in Madhya Pradesh, featuring cultural programs and adventure activities.
