Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple in Delhi, India, is a Bahá'í House of Worship known for its lotus-inspired design. Completed in 1986, its 27 marble petals symbolize unity. Open to all faiths, it offers a serene space for meditation and reflection in lush surroundings, making it a significant cultural and architectural landmark.
