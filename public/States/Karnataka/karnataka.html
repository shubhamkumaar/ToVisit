About

Karnataka, with its capital in Bengaluru, is a southern Indian state celebrated for its technological innovation and rich history. The state's culture is marked by a blend of traditions and classical art forms. Karnataka's diverse economy includes a strong presence in information technology, biotechnology, and manufacturing. Tourists are drawn to its historical sites, lush landscapes, and the Western Ghats. While managing rapid urbanization is a challenge, Karnataka continues to thrive as a hub of innovation and culture, offering a captivating subject for exploration and study.

Cultural Experiences

Witness the grand Mysore Dasara Festival, Explore the Mysore Palace, Attend traditional Yakshagana and Dollu Kunitha performances, Visit the Chitrasanthe art festival in Bangalore

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Kunitha

      Experience the vibrant and rhythmic Dollu Kunitha dance

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Mysuru Dasara

      A grand celebration of the victory of good over evil,.

    • Ugadi

      Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year with special dishes and the Panchangam reading.

    • Ganesh Chaturthi

      The birthday of Lord Ganesha is celebrated with great enthusiasm, including idol processions and traditional music and dance.

    • Hampi Utsav

      A cultural extravaganza set against the backdrop of the historic ruins of Hampi, featuring dance performances, music concerts, and a vibrant market.

    • Karaga Festival

      This unique and ancient Bengaluru festival involves a priest carrying an adorned Karaga pot on his head through the streets, symbolizing the Goddess Draupadi.
