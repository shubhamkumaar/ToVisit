Visiting Time

Mysore, a city located in the southern part of India, boasts a tropical wet and dry climate with warm temperatures throughout most of the year, except during the cool winter season. Therefore, the ideal time to visit Mysore is from October to February.

History

Mysore, the city with a rich history, was established by the Wadiyar dynasty's rulers. Its core was built around the magnificent Mysore Palace, which has remained the city's centerpiece throughout the centuries. Mysore flourished as a significant hub for trade and culture, especially renowned for its silk and sandalwood products.

Geography

Mysore is situated in the heart of Karnataka and covers an area of approximately 128.42 km2. The city is nestled within the Deccan Plateau, with an average elevation of around 770 m above sea level. Mysore is positioned at approximately 12.305° N latitude and 76.655° E longitude.

Weather

The city's annual mean temperature averages around 24.5 °C (76.1 °F), Summers which extend from March to June (exceed 37 °C (98.6 °F)), Winter(8 °C (46.4 °F)).

Features

•Rich Historical Heritage: Mysore has a captivating history, adorned with iconic landmarks such as the Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill, and St. Philomena's Church, showcasing its cultural and historical importance.

•Technology and Business Hub: Mysore is a bustling center for technology and commerce, featuring a growing presence of IT companies and startups. It's emerging as a significant contributor to India's tech industry, with areas like the Mysore IT Park fostering this growth.

• Culinary Delights: Mysore is celebrated for its distinctive and delectable cuisine, featuring the renowned Mysore Masala Dosa and a variety of mouthwatering dishes, creating a haven for food enthusiasts.
