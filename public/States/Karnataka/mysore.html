Mysore Palace

Mysore, in Karnataka, is steeped in cultural richness and historical splendor, epitomized by the magnificent Mysore Palace. The city is famed for its lively Dasara festival, featuring a grand procession and illuminated palace celebrations. Nestled near the Chamundi Hills, Mysore offers the majestic Chamundeshwari Temple and a well-regarded zoo. This blend of history, culture, and natural beauty makes Mysore a captivating destination in Southern India.

Festivals

Wangla

Wangala Festival of Meghalaya is a most popular festival among the Garos of Meghalaya, India. Wangala Festival is a harvest festival held in honour of Saljong, the Sun-god of fertility. The celebration of the Wangala Festival marks the end of a period of toil, which brings good output of the fields.
