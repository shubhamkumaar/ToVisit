Discover the Beauty of Tamil Nadu

Explore the rich heritage, spirituality, and adventure of Tamil Nadu

Heritage Sites

Explore the historical wonders of Tamil Nadu, including The Brihadeeswarar Temple and more.

  • Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur

  • Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai

  • Mamallapuram (Mahabalipuram) Group of Monuments

  • Chola Temples in Gangaikonda Cholapuram and Darasuram

Spiritual Journeys

Embark on spiritual adventures to famous Arunachaleswarar Temple, Ramanathaswamy Temple, and other sacred places.

  • Visit the Arunachaleswarar Temple in Tiruvannamalai

  • Seek blessings at the Ramanathaswamy Temple in Rameswaram

  • Explore the spiritual town of Kanchipuram

  • Meditate at the Ramana Maharshi Ashram in Tiruvannamalai

Cultural Experiences

Attend the Pongal festival celebrations, Witness classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi, Explore the DakshinaChitra heritage village, Visit the Government Museum in Chennai.

  • Culinary Delights

    • Savoury south Tiffins

      Savor South Indian cuisine with dosa, idli, and sambar

    • Tamil - Spicy dishes

      Try Chettinad cuisine with spicy dishes like Chettinad Chicken Curry

    • Savoury sweets

      Taste traditional sweets like Mysore Pak and Jangiri

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Bharatanatyam

      Experience Bharatanatyam, a classical dance form

    • Tamil - Music

      Listen to Carnatic music concerts

    • Folk

      Enjoy Tamil folk dances like Karakattam and Oyilattam

    • Melodious Musics

      Attend classical music festivals like the Chennai Music Season

  • Traditional Handicrafts

    • Sculptures

      Buy traditional bronze and stone sculptures.

    • Art - forms

      Explore the Tanjore (Thanjavur) painting art form

    • Saree art

      Shop for exquisite Kanchipuram silk sarees

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Pongal

      The harvest festival of Tamil Nadu celebrated with fervor and the preparation of a special dish called Pongal.

    • Diwali

      The Festival of Lights is celebrated with grandeur, featuring colorful decorations and the lighting of lamps.

    • Navratri

      A nine-night festival dedicated to Goddess Durga, marked by dance performances like Garba and Dandiya.

    • Tamil New Year (Puthandu)

      The Tamil New Year is celebrated with traditional rituals and feasting on special dishes.

    • Karthigai Deepam

      A festival of lights where lamps are lit in homes and temples, particularly in the town of Tiruvannamalai.
