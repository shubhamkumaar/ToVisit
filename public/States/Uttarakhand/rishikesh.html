Rishikesh

Rishikesh, nestled in the Uttarakhand region of India, is a spiritual haven along the Ganges River, surrounded by the Himalayas. Renowned for its ashrams and yoga retreats, it draws seekers and practitioners from diverse backgrounds. The Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula bridges add to its spiritual charm, while the town serves as a gateway to the sacred Char Dham pilgrimage. Adventure enthusiasts also flock to Rishikesh for activities like white-water rafting in its scenic surroundings.

Festivals

Wangla

Wangala Festival of Meghalaya is a most popular festival among the Garos of Meghalaya, India. Wangala Festival is a harvest festival held in honour of Saljong, the Sun-god of fertility. The celebration of the Wangala Festival marks the end of a period of toil, which brings good output of the fields.
