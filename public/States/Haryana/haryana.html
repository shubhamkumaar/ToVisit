  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Surajkund International Crafts Mela

      This is one of the most famous festivals in Haryana, held annually in Surajkund, near Faridabad. It features a wide range of handicrafts, traditional art forms, and cultural performances from across India and the world.

    • Baisakhi

      Baisakhi is celebrated with great enthusiasm in Haryana, especially in rural areas. It marks the beginning of the harvest season, and people participate in various cultural activities and traditional dances like Giddha and Bhangra.

    • Teej

      Teej is a monsoon festival celebrated predominantly by women. They dress in colorful traditional attire, fast, and participate in folk dances to celebrate the arrival of the monsoon season.

    • Guga Navami

      This festival is dedicated to Guga, a snake god, and is widely celebrated in the Bishnoi community of Haryana. People offer prayers, sing folk songs, and perform traditional dances.

    • Holi

      Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated with enthusiasm in Haryana. People play with colors, sing and dance to celebrate the arrival of spring.
