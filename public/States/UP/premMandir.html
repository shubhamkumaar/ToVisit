About Prem Mandir

Prem Mandir The Temple of Divine Love is a Hindu temple in Vrindavan Mathura district, Uttar Pradesh, India. The temple was established by Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj (the fifth Original Jagadguru).It is maintained by Jagadguru Kripalu Parishat, an international non-profit, educational, spiritual, charitable trust. The complex is on a 55-acre site on the outskirts of Vrindavan. It is dedicated to Radha Krishna and Sita Ram. Radha Krishna are on the first level and Sita Ram are on the second level. Different Leelas of Shri Krishna and Rasik saints are depicted all over the wall of the main temple.

History and Legend

Prem Mandir, situated in the holy city of Vrindavan, India, is a remarkable Hindu temple that holds deep significance for devotees of Lord Krishna and Radha. This architectural marvel was inaugurated on February 17, 2012, by the revered spiritual leader, Jagadguru Kripalu Maharaj. The name "Prem Mandir" translates to the "Temple of Love," and it lives up to its name by embodying the profound love between Lord Krishna and Radha. The temple's construction is a testament to the dedication and devotion of Jagadguru Kripalu Maharaj's followers. It is constructed entirely from imported Italian marble, which is meticulously carved with intricate designs and sculptures, portraying the life and pastimes of Lord Krishna. This painstaking craftsmanship brings the stories of Lord Krishna to life, making the temple a visual and spiritual masterpiece.

Architecture

Prem Mandir is renowned for its stunning white marble architecture. The temple complex features intricate carvings, domes, and spires, making it a magnificent piece of architectural art.

Things to do

  • Explore the Temple Complex:

    Take your time to explore the intricately designed temple complex, which includes beautifully carved walls, pillars, and domes. Admire the exquisite architecture and craftsmanship that went into creating the Prem Mandir.

  • Attend the Aarti:

    Witnessing the grand Aarti (devotional ceremony) at the Prem Mandir is a must-do experience. The Aarti takes place in the morning and evening and involves chanting, singing, and offering prayers to the deities. It is a mesmerizing and spiritually uplifting event.

  • Participate in Bhajans and Kirtans:

    Join the devotees in singing devotional songs, known as bhajans and kirtans. The uplifting music and chanting create a joyous atmosphere and help you connect with the divine.

  • Visit the Shyama Kunda and Radha Kunda:

    These sacred water bodies located within the temple complex hold great significance in Hindu mythology. Take a dip in the holy waters and offer your prayers for purification and blessings.

  • Enjoy the Musical Fountain Show:

    The Prem Mandir boasts a spectacular musical fountain show in the evening. Watch as colorful lights dance along with synchronized music, creating a captivating visual spectacle.

  • Capture Memorable Photos:

    The Prem Mandir provides numerous photo opportunities with its beautiful architecture, intricate details, and serene surroundings. Capture the memories of your visit with stunning photographs.

Message Of Love

Prem Mandir embodies the message of love and devotion, which is central to the worship of Lord Krishna. The temple's name, "Prem Mandir," emphasizes the importance of divine love.

Features

•Deities: The main deities enshrined in the temple are Lord Krishna and Radha. The temple is known for its beautifully adorned idols of the divine couple, depicting various leelas (divine pastimes) of Lord Krishna.

•Light and Sound Show: Prem Mandir hosts an impressive light and sound show in the evening. This multimedia show narrates the story of Lord Krishna and Radha, creating a mesmerizing experience for visitors.

• Prem Mandir Musical Fountain: The temple complex also includes a musical fountain that showcases a synchronized water, light, and music show. It adds to the grandeur of the place and is a popular attraction for tourists.
