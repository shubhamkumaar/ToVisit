About

Telangana, with its capital in Hyderabad, is a dynamic state in southern India known for its rich historical legacy, vibrant culture, and economic diversity. The state's cultural fabric is woven with the classical Telugu language, traditional arts like Kuchipudi dance, and lively festivals like Bonalu. The economy of Telangana is driven by a mix of sectors, including manufacturing, information technology, and agriculture. Tourist attractions in the state encompass the historic temples of Warangal, the lush landscapes of Adilabad, and the modern charm of Hyderabad.
