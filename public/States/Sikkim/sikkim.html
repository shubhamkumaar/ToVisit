Discover the Beauty of Sikkim

Explore the rich heritage, spirituality, and adventure of Sikkim

About

Sikkim, with its capital in Gangtok, is an enchanting northeastern Indian state celebrated for its Himalayan beauty, rich cultural heritage, and unique history. The culture is deeply rooted in Tibetan and Nepalese traditions, with Buddhism playing a central role in festivals like Losar and Dashain. Agriculture, particularly cardamom and organic farming, sustains the economy, and eco-tourism is promoted. Tourist attractions include the serene Tsomgo Lake, ancient monasteries like Rumtek and Pemayangtse, and Himalayan treks. Sikkim faces challenges in preserving its ecological balance, infrastructure development, and improving healthcare access in remote areas. It remains a captivating subject for exploration and study.

Gangtok

Gangtok is often considered one of the best cities to visit. It's the capital and the largest town in the state, known for its beautiful scenery, including the Himalayan mountains, Buddhist monasteries, and vibrant culture.

Embark on spiritual adventures to Enchey Monastery, Tashiding Monastery, and other sacred places.
