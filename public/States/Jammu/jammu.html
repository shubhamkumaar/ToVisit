Discover the Beauty of Jammu & Kashmir

Explore the rich heritage, spirituality, and adventure of Jammu & Kashmir

Capital : Srinagar

Jammu and Kashmir, with its capital in Srinagar (summer) and Jammu (winter), is a northern Indian union territory known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse culture. The region's economy relies on agriculture, horticulture, and tourism, offering visitors picturesque landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and serene lakes. Jammu and Kashmir faces unique challenges related to security and political autonomy and aims to promote peace and prosperity in the region. Its breathtaking scenery and complex political landscape make it a captivating area for in-depth analysis and research.

    • Fairs and Festivals

      • Baisakhi

        Celebrated in April, Baisakhi marks the harvest festival with vibrant dance and music.

      • Hemis Festival

        This famous Buddhist festival in Hemis Monastery celebrates the birth of Guru Padmasambhava with masked dances.

      • Tulip Festival

        Located in Srinagar, this festival showcases the breathtaking beauty of tulip gardens in spring.

      • Lohri

        Celebrated in January, Lohri is a bonfire festival symbolizing the end of winter and the onset of spring.

      • Shikara Festival

        A celebration of the iconic Shikara boats in Dal Lake, it features boat races and cultural performances.
