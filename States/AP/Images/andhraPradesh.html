Cultural Experiences

Attend the Deccan Festival in Hyderabad, Explore the Kuchipudi village, known for the traditional Kuchipudi dance form, Enjoy the Lepakshi Dance and Music Festival

  • Culinary Delights

    • Chicken curry

      Savor Andhra cuisine known for its spicy dishes like Andhra Chicken Curry and Gongura Pickle

    • Varieties in Breakfast

      Try Pesarattu and Upma in the local breakfast menu

    • Savoury sweets

      Enjoy sweets like Pootharekulu and Ariselu

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Kuchipudi

      Experience Kuchipudi, a classical dance form

    • Telugu Folk

      Enjoy traditional Telugu folk music and songs

    • Dance forms

      Watch Perini Thandavam, a martial dance form

    • Melodious Musics

      Listen to the melodious strains of classical Carnatic music

  • Traditional Handicrafts

    • Kalamkari hand-painted textiles

      Indulge in the exquisite world of Kalamkari hand-painted textiles, where artistry and tradition seamlessly intertwine.

    • Wooden-Toys

      Explore famous Kondapalli wooden toys and crafts

    • Saree art

      Buy Mangalagiri and Gadwal silk sarees

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Sankranti

      Sankranti is a major harvest festival celebrated with colorful kite flying and traditional foods.

    • Ugadi

      Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year with special dishes and the Panchangam reading.

    • Bonalu

      Bonalu is a folk festival honoring the goddess Mahakali, celebrated with music and offerings.

    • Pongal

      Pongal is a Tamil festival celebrated in Andhra Pradesh with fervor, thanking the sun god for a bountiful harvest.

    • Deccan Festival

      This festival showcases the rich culture and heritage of Andhra Pradesh through dance, music, and crafts.

Adventure and Nature

Experience thrilling activities like river rafting, trekking, and bird watching in Andhra's natural wonders.

  • Trekking in the Eastern Ghats, including the Araku Valley and Papikondalu

    • Location: Koshi River, Saharsa District

    • Adventure Level: Moderate to Challenging

    • Description: Experience the thrill of river rafting in the mighty Koshi River. Navigate through the river's rapids and enjoy the scenic beauty surrounding the area.

  • Visit the scenic Borra Caves in Araku

    • Location: West Champaran District

    • Adventure Level: Easy to Moderate

    • Description: Explore Valmiki National Park, one of the most biodiverse national parks in India. Go on a safari to spot Bengal tigers, leopards, elephants, and various bird species. The park also houses the famous Valmiki Ashram.

  • Explore the lush forests and wildlife of Nagarjuna Sagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve

    • Location: Rajgir, Nalanda District

    • Adventure Level: Moderate

    • Description: Embark on a trekking adventure in the picturesque hills of Rajgir. The trails offer stunning views, and you can visit sites like Griddhakuta Peak and Vulture's Peak during your trek

  • Relax on the pristine beaches of Visakhapatnam and Kakinada

    • Location: Bhagalpur District

    • Adventure Level: Easy

    • Description: Explore the Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary on a bike. Enjoy the scenic ride along the Ganges River and keep an eye out for the endangered Gangetic dolphins in their natural habitat.
