Cultural Experiences

Odissi classical dance performances, Raghurajpur artist village for Pattachitra art, Attend local festivals like Raja Parba

  • Culinary Delights

    • Cuisine dishes

      Odia cuisine featuring dishes like Dalma, Pakhala, and Rasgulla

    • Savoury sweets

      Chenna Poda (a famous dessert)

    • Street-Foods

      Street food in Cuttack, including Dahibara Aloodum

  • Folk Dances and Music

    • Dance forms

      Witness Gotipua dance performances

    • Melodies

      Enjoy traditional Odia music with instruments like the Mardala and Ghungroo

  • Traditional Handicrafts

    • Exciting-Textiles

      Shop for exquisite Sambalpuri and Ikat textiles

    • Silver-filigree

      Explore silver filigree work in Cuttack

    • Crafts-Toys

      Buy intricately designed Dokra metal crafts

  • Fairs and Festivals

    • Ratha Yatra

      The grand chariot festival in Puri where Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra are taken on a procession in elaborately decorated chariots.

    • Durga Puja

      Celebrated with great fervor, it's a major festival where people worship Goddess Durga.

    • Konark Dance Festival

      An annual cultural extravaganza featuring classical and folk dance performances at the Konark Sun Temple.

    • Raja Parba

      A celebration of womanhood and fertility, it involves rituals and activities highlighting the importance of women in agriculture.

    • Kalinga Mahotsav

      An event that celebrates martial arts and showcases traditional forms of combat like 'Paika Akhada.
